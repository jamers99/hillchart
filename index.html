How to Use

Set a title: Click the title field and type your chart name

Add scopes: Click "+ Add Scope" and enter a name

Move scopes: Drag scopes left to right on the hill

Edit scopes: Double-click a scope to rename or delete it

Share: Click "Copy Link" to get a shareable URL

Export: Click "Copy Image" to copy a PNG to your clipboard
